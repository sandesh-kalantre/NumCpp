List of predefined constants which can be used

Napier's constant 
Syntax: math.e

Pi
Syntax: math.pi

Golden ratio
Syntax: math.phi

Speed of light
Syntax: phy.c

Magnetic constant/Permeability of free space
Syntax: phy.mu_0

Electric constant/Permittivity of free space
Syntax: phy.epsilon_0

Planck constant
Syntax: phy.h

Reduced Planck constant/H-bar/H-cut
Syntax: phy.hbar or phy.hcut (both return the same value)

Gravitational constant
Syntax: phy.G

Acceleration on the surface of the Earth due to gravity
Syntax: phy.g

Magnitude of charge on an electron
Syntax: phy.e

Gas constant
Syntax: phy.R

Fine-structure constant/Sommerfeld's constant
Syntax: phy.alpha

Avagadro's number
Syntax: phy.N_A

Boltzmann's constant
Syntax: phy.k

Stefan's constant
Syntax: phy.sigma

Wein's constant
Syntax: phy.Wein

Rydberg's constant
Syntax: phy.Rydberg

Mass of an electron
Syntax: phy.m_e

Mass of a proton
Syntax: phy.m_p

Mass of a neutron
Syntax: phy.m_n
